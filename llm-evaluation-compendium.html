Awesome LLM Evaluation

A comprehensive compendium of evaluation methods for large language models and LLM-based systems — curated for academics and industry professionals.

⭐ GitHub Repository 📚 LLM Search & Recommender ▶ SF Big Analytics Talk ▶ Analytics Vidhya ▶ Data Phoenix Mar'24 👤 Andrei Lopatenko

The aim of this compendium is to assist academics and industry professionals in creating effective evaluation suites tailored to their specific needs. It reviews the top industry practices for assessing large language models (LLMs) and their applications — going beyond merely cataloging benchmarks, encompassing a comprehensive overview of all effective and practical evaluation techniques, including those embedded within papers that primarily introduce new LLM methodologies and tasks.

This resource enables anyone to easily find information needed for evaluating an LLM or LLM application for specific tasks, determining the best assessment methods, or understanding how well an LLM performs in a particular domain. It also highlights various methods for evaluating the evaluation tasks themselves, to ensure evaluations align with business or academic objectives.
